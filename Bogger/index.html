Bogger Game

Game goal:

Avoid the girls on the path, pass the river by stepping on the back of the bugs, save the princess and bring her back. Remember to collect the key first, which is necessary to be used to unlock the cage that imprison the princess. Princess will slow you down when you hold her, so release her when necessary and grab her again when chances come. Collect gems for extra score.

How to play:

Press arrow key to move. Press e to hold or release the princess.
